About Me

My name is Vishvesh Dave and this is my portfolio. For vegetarian Vishvesh Dave, the options before this year oftentimes left him brown-bagging it at lunch. His whole family follows a vegetarian diet, going back to great-great grandparents, he said. Before, lunch took a lot more planning. Not so much now. "I don't have to make my lunch (every day) because I know there's something at school," said Dave, a sophomore at John Marshall. "Before, I had nothing to eat at lunch and I would always have to bring from home."
